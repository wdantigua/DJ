(Don't) Make More Nature

Pristine nature does not exist so why are we going out of our ways to manufacture natural spaces. This type of spaces only create a false “look but don’t interact” approach to nature for the sake of not making it look “ugly.” Not only that but it prevents novel ecosystems from thriving. As Emma Marris, writer and environmentalist states “let children touch nature.” A prominent example resides right on the corners of Chelsea on the form of The Highline
